# Problem

### Statement
Given a tree with N number of nodes and E number of edges, find the number of subtrees that can be formed with each node center and R as radius, without repetition in tree structures.
### Input Format:
- R
- N
- E
- Edges - line by line
 
### Test Cases
1. 
    #### Input:
    - 1
    - 7
    - 6
    - 1 2 <br>
      1 3 <br>
      1 4 <br>
      1 5 <br>
      2 6 <br>
      2 7
    #### Output:
    - 3
2.
    #### Input:
    - 1 
    - 4
    - 3
    - 1 2 <br>
      1 3 <br>
      1 4 <br>
    #### Output:
    - 2
