# Problem

### Statement
Given a linked list of length N, split the linked list into two with alternate nodes.

### Input format:
- N (Number of input nodes)
- List of N integers

### Test Cases
1.
    #### Input:
    - 7
    - 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
    #### Output:
    - 1 3 5 7
    - 2 4 6
2.
    #### Input:
    - 4
    - 1 4 5 6
    #### Output:
    - 1 5
    - 4 6
3.
    #### Input:
    - 8
    - 6 7 5 3 9 2 4 1
    #### Output:
    - 6 5 9 4
    - 7 3 2 1
